Dr. M P Pushpalata

Currently (in 2020), Head of the department of Computer Science and Engineering, with nearly three decades of teaching experience. Passionate and committed to inspire students to pursue academic and personal excellence. Driven by the motto, “Anything worth doing is worth doing well,” to create a challenging and engaging learning environment for students and self.
